Exterior Treatments

Each service will begin with an inspection to determine the course of action for your home. After the inspection the service will be customized for your home. Often times the service will include:

  • full de-webbing of eaves, around windows, and doors.
  • full perimeter spray around the house, shop, and garage.
  • Crack and crevice treatment for mice and other insects that might be nesting inside your walls.
  • Exterior granulation around flower beds and in mulch areas around the property.
  • Spot treatment of specialty areas around the property.

Interior Treatments

Interior treatments are not often necessary, but when pests are especially evident in the home we will treat inside. This treatment will include these services:

  • Garage sticky traps/rodent traps.
  • Garage crack and crevice service.
  • Crack and crevice service around sinks, doors, baseboards, and windows.
  • Ant bait applications.
